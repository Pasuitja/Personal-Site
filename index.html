About Jake

The Full Picture

I'm Jake Pasuit — currently studying Games & Interactive Media at Michigan State University, and I've spent the past few years doing something most early-career candidates can't claim: working across both B2B and B2C, in real roles, with real results.

On the B2B side, I worked with TechStak Marketing — a go-to-market marketing firm serving technology providers — developing AI-powered content strategies, building websites, producing AI-generated videos with HeyGen and ElevenLabs, and running cold email campaigns using Instantly.ai. On the B2C side, I ran digital marketing and social commerce for Unleashed Life, a consumer brand where I built TikTok Shops and Facebook Shops from scratch, managed Shopify catalogs, and created UGC content that converted.

What sets me apart isn't just the tools — it's the range. I can sit in a GTM strategy session in the morning and shoot a UGC video in the afternoon. I'm remote-native, self-directed, and bring the discipline of a former high school athlete to every project.
